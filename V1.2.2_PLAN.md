# Chatty Critter — v1.2.2 Plan
**Date:** 2026-05-15  
**Input:** Full player experience review (9-year-old perspective) + QA audit  
**Theme:** *"Make every moment feel magical"*

---

## 🎮 What the Player Review Told Us

Playing as a young player, here's the honest experience of v1.2:

> *"The pet is really cute and the chat makes me laugh. But after 30 minutes I kinda ran out of things to do. The stats go down too fast and I felt stressed. I want more surprises! And I really want to HUG my pet."*

**Top 3 emotional moments that landed well:**
- First time the pet responds in its personality — genuine surprise and delight
- Catching a ball combo — satisfying and exciting
- Feeding the pet and seeing the food float up — visually rewarding

**Top 3 drop-off points:**
- Stat decay felt punishing — players quit when everything is red
- No way to physically interact with the pet (just chat)
- After levelling up a few times, nothing new to discover

### Shipped Now:

| Feature | What it does |

## 🗺️ Roadmap: Features to Build Next

### Priority 1 — Deepen the Bond (v1.3)

| # | Feature | Description | Effort |
|---|---------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | **Pet Tricks** | Tap pet 3× fast → it does a trick (spin, backflip, star burst). Teach new tricks at milestone levels. Tricks have names and unlock as rewards | M |
| 2 | **Time of Day System** | Home screen background and pet behavior change with real clock: Dawn (6-9am), Day (9am-5pm), Dusk (5-8pm), Night (8pm-6am). Pet is sleepier at night, extra energetic at noon | S |
| 3 | **Pet Weekly Letter** | Once a week, a little envelope icon appears. Open it to read a "handwritten" letter from your pet — references recent topics, current level, number of days as friends. Opens with a paper-unfolding animation | M |

### Priority 2 — More to Discover (v1.3)

| # | Feature | Description | Effort |
|---|---------|-------------|--------|
| 4 | **Achievement Badges** | 20 achievements to unlock ("First Chat", "100 Catches", "Level 5", "Full Happiness" etc.). Badge collection on Profile screen. Unlocking plays fanfare + banner | M |
| 5 | **Bubble Pop Mini-Game** | Bubbles float up from the bottom. Tap to pop before they escape! Golden bubbles = bonus coins. Speed increases every 10 pops. Different from Catch Ball (reflex vs anticipation) | M |
| 6 | **Pet Evolution Visual** | At Level 5, 10, 15 — the pet SVG gets a small visual upgrade (sparkle aura, crown, wings). Just CSS filter + overlay. Big emotional milestone moment | S |

### Priority 3 — World Building (v1.4)

| # | Feature | Description | Effort |
|---|---------|-------------|--------|
| 7 | **Decorate the Room** | Background in Home screen becomes a "room". Buy furniture (couch, plant, rug, window) from Shop. Items placed as emoji layers over the background | L |
| 8 | **Seasonal Events** | Every month a themed event runs: Valentine hearts in Feb, Easter eggs in April, etc. Special food items, outfits, and chat responses for the season | M |
| 9 | **Photo Booth** | Tap a 📷 button to "take a photo" of your dressed-up pet. Downloads as a PNG with a cute Chatty Critter frame. Kids can share with friends | M |
| 10 | **Pet vs Robot** | A "Challenge" mode where your pet's stats battle a randomly generated robot pet. Win = coins. Lose = pep talk from your pet. No multiplayer needed | L |

## 🎨 Design Principles Going Forward

1. **Every tap should feel satisfying** — sound + animation on every interaction ✅ (done in v1.2.2)
2. **The pet should feel alive, not maintained** — less stat management, more spontaneous moments
3. **Surprises > Features** — one unexpected thing (letter, trick, evolution) beats 5 predictable ones
4. **Keep the phone in landscape heart** — every feature should work one-handed on a phone
5. **Make kids want to show their friends** — sharable moments (photo booth, level name, best outfit)
